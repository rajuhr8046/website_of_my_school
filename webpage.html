What Is School?

A school is an educational institution designed to provide learning spaces and learning environments for the teaching of students (or "pupils") under the direction of teachers.

What Is High School?

In India, high school is a grade of education from Standards IX(9) to X(10). Standards IX(9) and X(10) are also called Secondary School. Usually, students from ages 14 to 17 study in this section. These schools may be affiliated to national boards (like CBSE,ISC, and NIOS) or various state boards.
